Alpha Tahoe: Our mission

In creating a base for new ecological development and elimination of particle debris in Lake Tahoe, we are organizing a new world of community efforts

A local Eco-base opens up multiple important economic avenues:

  • Engaged, aware tourists
  • Volunteer industries
  • refueling stations

More importantly, it will also open the door to unlocking future lake exploration:

  • assess the health impacts of tarnishing the lake
  • learn how to build and operate a base that engages the community to create improvement
  • test educational methods and outreach opportunities

We will learn about living in microecology, surviving in spite of human activity, and more, all while staying close enough to natural lake features for easy communication and assistance if needed.
